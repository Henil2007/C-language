STEP-3 : HOT ALL THE THINKS FOR SOME TIME
STEP-4 : AFTER SOME TIME CHELK THE TEA COLOR OF THE TEA CHANGES TO BROWN OR NOT
STEP-5 : IF NOT BROWN THEN GO TO STEP-4 ELSE GO TO STEP-7
STEP-6 : SERVE THE TEA IN A CUP
STEP-7 : END
